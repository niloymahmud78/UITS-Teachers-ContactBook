Story Behind Making This Site

This site is for the UITS students. Oneday I saw my cousin is searching for an teacher's contact number or information in a doc file. And it took a lot of time to find the contact. So I decided to make a site that will have all the teacther's contact & Information in one place, And it will have a searchbar. So whenever an student will search the name of a teacher, his full information and contact details will be visible.
